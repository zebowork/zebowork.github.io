Free application for laundry in your area

ZEBO app will search laundries in your area, you can place job to laundry which suites you and your needs. For more information see our about page and youtube channel. It is really simple, in 3 steps.

  1. Add laundry into your basket

  2. Select your Zebo (laundry)

  3. Post your job

You can also ask your favorite zebo (laundry) to come for pickup without adding any items.

  1. Add zebo (laundry) to your favorite

  2. Click on favorite icon

  3. Ask 4 pickup & post
