Items

Wide range of items available. You can select any item to put it into your basket. You can add your desire operations on item, by the way you can select what is the febric of your item to help laundry treat it like wise. Put your item pictures to your future reference.



Basket

Add items into your basket and post them whenever you want. You can edit items from basket or remove them if you like. you can empty your basket at once with single click. Put your address, so we can find all laundries near your area. You can also select your service type (Express, urgent or standard)


Price

When you click check price button from basket, ZEBO will show you summaries (price + rating) of all the available laundries in your area. You can click on detail price to check price break down.


Easy to use

Simple interfaces, minimum steps to place the job and intuitive to use. You don't require to register yourself to use ZEBO. If you create account, you will have some extra benefits e-g history etc
