Cookie Policy for Zebo

This is the Cookie Policy for Zebo, accessible from zebo.work/cookies.html

What Are Cookies

Cookies are tiny files that are downloaded to your computer, to improve your experience. This page describes what information they gather, how we use it and why we sometimes need to store these cookies.

For more general information on cookies and how to disable them, please read "What Are Cookies".

Disabling Cookies

You can prevent the setting of cookies by adjusting the settings on your browser (see your browser Help for how to do this). Be aware that disabling cookies will affect the functionality of this and many other websites that you visit. Disabling cookies will usually result in also disabling certain functionality and features of the sites. Therefore it is recommended that you do not disable cookies.

How We Use Cookies

We donot use cookies (for now) because unfortunately in most cases there are no industry standard options for disabling cookies without completely disabling the functionality and features they can add to site.

The Cookies We Set

We don't set any cookie at all, but in future we might set Login related cookies

  • Login related cookies

    Use case of these cookies is when you are logged in so that we can remember this fact. This prevents you from having to log in every single time you visit a new page. These cookies are typically removed or cleared when you log out to ensure that you can only access restricted features and areas when logged in.

  • We use following instead of cookies to make site fast and working. These all are safe you can serach about them on internet or contact us for more details

    1. Local & session storage
    2. IndexedDB
    3. Service workers & cache

Third Party Cookies
